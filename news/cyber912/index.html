We placed second in the Cyber 9/12 Strategy Challenge.

April 22, 2022

2022

The Cyber 9/12 Strategy Challenge is an annual cyber policy competition for students across the globe. Teams compete to develop national and international security policy recommendations to tackle a fictional cyber incident. In partnership with the Atlantic Council, the Geneva Centre for Security Policy (GCSP) hosts one of the eight annual Cyber 9/12 Strategy Challenges.

This year I have the pleasure to team up with my colleagues Francesco Intoci, Lucas Rollet, Elvric Tromber, and our coach Stefanie Matter. We Team Lémanners placed 2nd among 32 international teams in this three-round competition.

I would like to thank the GCSP and the Atlantic Council for hosting the Cyber 9/12 Strategy Challenge 2022 in Geneva. Special mention to Cyber Group - ETH Student Initiative for trainings, supports, and all the fun along this wonderful journey. Last but not least, big thanks to my teammates, our coach, competition judges, speech guests, and all the peer competitors!
